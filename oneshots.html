One-Shot Adventures

The Skeletons have long guarded their master, but they long for an adventure of their own! Rumor has it a great treasure is in the nearby Human Dungeon, a.k.a. the village of Westhaven. Will the skeletons claim their prize and become adventurers of renown?

Four brave Koroks have finished the Korok Trials and now seek greater adventure. Can they brave the long forgotten Forest Temple and complete the Legendary Korok Trial?
