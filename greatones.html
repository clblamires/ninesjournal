--- layout: greatones title: The Tales of the Great Ones permalink: /the-great-ones/ ---

The adventures and stories of unlikely companions who find themselves together for a yet unknown purpose. Their journey takes them through the continent of Caladmein, an unforgiving land with trials around every corner. The party met in Oakshade, a small hamlet near the Redstone Canyons, and were inspired to travel together to save a lowly farmer from creatures lurking in shadow.
