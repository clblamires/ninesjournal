I honestly do not know what we are doing in this world right now. What is our purpose? What is my purpose? I suppose it does not matter as long as we are alive. This is a list of our current tasks and the things we need to do to make them happen, as far as we understand it.

Number Nine

Main Quests

  • A Sign of Good Luck

    Ezio saw a Simurgh while he watched over our camp, and has a rainbow feather to prove it. Exatorus seemed particularly interested in finding and studying the creature, especially since it is not only a rare sight, but also unexpectedly far away from desert regions.

    Quest given by Exatorus

    In Progress...

  • Saviors

    Escort the Drider's captives back to Oakshade.

    Complete Reward: GP

  • Hebo's Friend

    The farmer is missing and likely has been taken by whatever giant creature ran through the farm. We offered to help Hebo find and rescue his friend. The farmer is safe now, and we'll escort him back into town.

    Quest given by Exatorus and Hebo

    Complete Reward: Friendship

  • Following the Trail

    Something attacked Hebo's farm, and Exatorus wants us to find it. He prefers that we bring it back to him alive, but that might not be possible. We found it underneath the plains surrounded by Kraul.

    Quest given by Exatorus

    Complete Reward: Level Up

  • Bring It to Me Alive

    Exatorus wanted it alive. We couldn't guarantee it would survive if we found it. And it didn't. I ended up overkilling it by blowing its head off. We scavanged its legs to provide the wizard with a sample, but the rest of the body of the drow spider will rot in the tunnels below the sun.

    Quest given by Exatorus

    Failed Reward: Spider sword

  • Capturing Monsters

    Exatorus wanted a sample of the monster or monsters that attacked Hebo's farm. As it turned out, these were phase spiders. Oromis, being the brute that he is, lifted and hauled the spider's dead body to Exatorus himself.

    Quest given by Exatorus

    Complete Reward: GP

  • Defend the Farm

    Phase spiders had been attacking Hebo's farm, and he asked us to join him in defending it. We successfully killed the monsters, but not before they killed the animals and wrecked both the barn and the farmhouse.

    Quest given by Hebo

    Failed

  • The Wizard

    Hebo went to visit the wizard Exatorus to find out more about the strange happenings on his farm. We found the wizard outside of the town halfway up into the mountains.

    Quest given by everyone

    Complete
