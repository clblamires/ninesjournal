My name is Casey and I'm a professor of web development. This site, Number Nine's Blog, was built for my Dungeons and Dragons group. The seven of us (six plus the DM) all have busy lives, but we try to get together at least once or twice a month to play. None of us have much experience, except for Luna and the DM.

This website was built with HTML5, CSS3, AngularJS, Jekyll, Github, and Netlify. The design of the page is all my own, with some Font Awesome thrown in.

I teach Web Design, Computer Programming, and App Development at Lewis-Clark State College. I built this site to see if I could make an entire site in half an hour. It's not perfect, but it's functional. My students and I often take on simple projects for experience and portfolio-building, and our rates are reasonable. Let me know if you have questions!
