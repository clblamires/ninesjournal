Main Quests

  • Find and rescue the farmer

    The farmer is missing and may have been taken by whatever giant creature ran through the farm. We offered to help Hebo find and rescue his friend. The farmer is safe now, and we'll escort him back into town.

    Quest given by Exatorus and Hebo

    Complete Reward: Friendship

  • Find the monster that destroyed Hebo's Farm

    Something attacked Hebo's farm, and Exatorus wants us to find it. He prefers that we bring it back to him alive, but that might not be possible. We found it underneath the plains surrounded by Kraul.

    Quest given by Exatorus

    Complete Reward: Level Up

  • Capture the monster that destroyed Hebo's Farm

    Exatorus wanted it alive. We couldn't guarantee it would survive if we found it. And it didn't. I ended up overkilling it by blowing its head off. We scavanged its legs to provide the wizard with a sample, but the rest of the body of the drow spider will rot in the tunnels below the sun.

    Quest given by Exatorus

    Failed Reward: Spider sword

  • Bring the farm monster to Exatorus

    Exatorus wanted a sample of the monster or monsters that attacked Hebo's farm. As it turned out, these were phase spiders. Oromis, being the brute that he is, lifted and hauled the spider's dead body to Exatorus himself.

    Quest given by Exatorus

    Complete Reward: GP
