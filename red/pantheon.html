The Pantheon

{% include return.html content="red" %}

Altee

Goddess of love and peace

Carond

Worshipped by those in the south (Tielith)

Demar

God of learning and building, mostly worshipped by the dwarves

Sebastian

God of cunning and trickery

Vanta

God of wars and battle

Chreo

God of the natural order, associated with time and death

Dauch

God of fortune and prize

Covea

God of nature, helping creatures thrive in their respective spheres

Phibea

God of darkness, also called the 'Spider'

Valtare

Goddess of warmth and light, Red's patron

Solutte

God of dominion and power
