The first campaign that takes place in the far away region of Caladmein. It follow the tales of a group of unlikely comrades who are challenged at every turn and find themselves discouraged along the way. This is written in the voice of one of the members, a Warforged named Number Nine.

This is the adventure in Calatia, a country far north of Hyrule, a land of ruins and ice. It is a Legend of Zelda themed campaign.

Sometimes our group runs one-shots, little single-session adventures in various different places.
