Maps of Calatia

Maps of the country of Calatia (and its surrounding areas) will be revealed as the Hyrulean Mercenaries explore it.

Loftwing Province

  • Mabel Village: a small fishing village to the far west
  • Mysterious Woods: a strange and thick forest north of Mabel Village with only a small path leading through
  • Tail Cave: a fishing cave south of Mabel. It is currently closed to the public
  • Edgewater Bay
  • Featherwood Forest: a thick and lush forest in the center of the province. Lately it is haunted by monsters at night
  • Spring of Wellness: a magical spring deep in the Featherwood. It is run by Bokoblins and has a campground.
  • Hokani Mountains: the mountain range separating the Loftwing Province from the north. Its caves are inhabited by monsters.
  • Hokani Lowlands
  • Toronbo Cape
  • Toronbo Shores: the shores that overlook the Great Sea.
  • Southern Lighthouse: the magical lighthouse that is supposed to keep the darkness at bay. It is run by a single caretaker.
  • Loftwing Steppe: the large grasslands between the southern shore and the Skygarden Mountains
  • Skygarden
  • Martha's Bay
  • Oldtown
